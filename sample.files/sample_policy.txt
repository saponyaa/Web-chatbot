Refund Policy:

1. Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
2. To request a refund, contact our support team with your order number.
3. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
4. Certain items such as gift cards and downloadable content are non-refundable.
5. Refund processing may take 5–10 business days.

Shipping Policy:

1. Orders are processed within 2 business days.
2. Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days.
3. Express shipping is available at an additional cost.
4. Tracking information will be provided once the order is shipped.
5. We currently ship only within the European Union.
